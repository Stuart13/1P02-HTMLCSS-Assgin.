Christmas Present Ideas

  1. A working TARDIS
  2. More milk because I just ran out
  3. A new retainer, this one hurts my teeth


The reason I decided I wanted a working TARDIS is because it could bring me back to the good old days when I didn't have a care in the world. It could also bring me back to times when I was on vacation, just so I could experience them all over again.

Personally, I would head back to Disney World in 2010 .

I went with a close friend of mine believe it or not and it was a whole lot of fun. We went on the Tower of Terror (before it was shut down) and other rides such as Space Mountain . I'd probably also use the TARDIS to explore space and all that fun stuff but I'm pretty sure my priorities are in check when I say Disney World comes first.



Other Ideas That Didn't Make The List

  • A puppy
  • Another puppy
  • A cat because a third puppy is a crowd




A Picture of a Welsh Terrier

The reason behind not placing any pets on my main list is simply because I think I'm aiming too high . I believe that my parents would start off small with maybe a guinea pig or a goldfish just to see whether I am responsible enough to take care of a pet. Unfortunately, I hate fish because they're slimy and I hate guinea pigs because they're not dogs or cats. These opinions put me in quite the unfortunate position, so I'll just have to convince my parents sometime before Christmas to let me get 3 pets all at once . I heard somewhere that if you get a pet on christmas you'll treat it more like a present than a living being, so I'm thinking maybe I should wait a bit just so I don't fall under the same pretenses.
